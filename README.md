# Hi, I'm Agustín! 🎮✨

I am a **Game Developer & Technical Artist** specialized in bridging the gap between complex code and visual assets. My expertise spans from architecting advanced gameplay systems and networking to creating production-ready 2D/3D art and animations. 

I focus on building scalable systems, immersive artificial intelligence, and fluid multiplayer experiences, leveraging AI tools to optimize my development workflow.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack & Expertise

### 🚀 Game Programming
* **Unreal Engine (C++ & Blueprints):** Architecture of advanced AI systems (Behavior Trees/State Machines), multiplayer networking, gameplay mechanics, modular UI systems, landscape design, and animation rigging/blueprints.
* **Unity (C#):** Full-cycle gameplay programming, multi-agent AI implementation, custom UI development, and multiplayer integration.

### 🎨 Art & Asset Pipelines
* **2D Art & Vector Illustration:** High-fidelity digital painting and concept art in Paint Tool SAI 2, alongside precise vector asset creation in Adobe Illustrator.
* **Pixel Art & Animation:** Comprehensive sprite sheet creation, character animation, and seamless tileset design using Aseprite and Adobe Animate.
* **3D Environment & Low-Poly Modeling:** Hard-surface and stylized 3D asset modeling in Blender and specialized retro/grid-based modeling in Crocotile3D.
